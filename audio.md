Presented here are over twelve hours of edited audio dictations used in the creation of *Bethlehem... to Seagrove*.

While these recordings served as the primary source material, the final book was expanded and refined with factual corrections and additional context.

These recordings now stand as a historical legacy, offering a unique opportunity to hear the narrative in the author's own voice.

(You can also play these audio recordings on [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLPsmBG-oOaDVC3ubwOZ3wqLXS3bQwh7EL).)
